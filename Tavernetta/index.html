Benvenuti

A Roman table
in the heart
of Denver.

Step through our doors and the world narrows to the scrape of a chair, the pop of a cork, the drift of pancetta and sage from the open kitchen. This is Tavernetta — an Italian neighborhood trattoria re-imagined beneath the vaulted bones of Union Station.

Our pasta is rolled before the sun crosses the platform. Our wine list travels from Alto Adige to Sicilia and stops to visit old friends along the way. Our hospitality is the kind your nonna would recognize — unhurried, generous, genuine.

La Famiglia

A legacy of Italian hospitality.

Tavernetta is brought to you by the James Beard Award-winning team behind Frasca Food & Wine — stewards of Italian cuisine in Colorado for more than two decades, and champions of the quiet excellence that defines a great trattoria.

Our philosophy is stubborn and simple: find the producer who cares the most, pay them fairly, and let the ingredient lead. Then pair it with a wine that has something to say.

"Tavernetta isn't trying to impress you — it's trying to feed you the way someone's grandmother would, if her grandmother happened to live in Trastevere." — Westword · Denver's Best Italian

La Cantina

A wine list
that speaks Italian.

Curated by the same team behind Frasca's award-winning cellar, our list is a love letter to the Italian peninsula — deep verticals from Piemonte, rare bottles from Alto Adige, and a growing roster of natural producers from Etna and the Aeolian islands.

Our sommeliers live for the pairing you'd never have ordered yourself. Ask for their current obsession.
